Earth Hour Mode

  • Use LED lights

    • last longer than traditional light bulbs
    • use up to 80% less electricity
    • durable and eco-friendly

  • Wash at a cold temperature

    • reduces the need to heat water
    • helps garments to last longer
    • cold water is gentler on fabrics, prevents shrinkage and fading

  • Turn off lights when not in use

    • extends the lifespan of the bulbs
    • saves energy, reducing electricity bills
    • promotes sustainability by conserving resources

  • Unplug devices when not in use

    • prevents "phantom energy" consumption
    • extends the lifespan of devices, protecting them from power surges
    • saves money on electricity bills, reducing overall energy waste

  • Keep thermostat at a low temp

    • leading to lower heating costs
    • reduced carbon footprint
    • decreases the strain on heating systems, extending their lifespan

  • Use solar powered devices

    • eeduces reliance on non-renewable energy sources
    • helps lower greenhouse gas emissions and combat climate change
    • harnessing free energy from the sun, decreases electricity demand from the grid

  • Switch Off

    Turn off your lights for one hour.

  • Join the Movement

    Be part of a global community.
